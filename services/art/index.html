About Commission Progress:

Please understand that I am a full-time college student and have the right to turn down a commission for any reason, including, but not limited to, the following:

  • Schedule/ time constraints
  • Illness and fatigue
  • Your request violates any of the above terms
  • Other emergencies

Additionally, please understand that it may take a long time to complete your commission due to any of the above reasons.

Customer’s Terms:

What you can do with your commission:

  • Reposting it WITH CREDIT and LINK back to any of my online profiles
  • Using the commission for non-commercial purposes (as long as credit is given)

What you can NOT do with your commission:

  • Reposting my art without giving me proper credit (meaning name and link back to one of my online profiles)
  • Claiming my art as your own.
  • Reselling it or using it commercially in any way to earn you money
  • Editing my artwork unless that was explicitly stated from the initial contact

Payments and Refunds:

  • I accept payments in USD via Paypal invoices.
  • I will contact you with further instructions.
  • All commissions are non-refundable unless there is a case of extreme emergency.
  • Commissions that are refunded are only refunded at 50% of the cost of the commission value. (I.e., if the commission was worth $10, you would only get back $5.)
  • 50% must be paid upfront by the commissioner before I can begin working on your commission and add your name to the commission queue.
  • The remaining 50% will be paid before the commission is completed after the commissioner approves of an unfinished watermarked version of the work.
  • If the commissioner does not like it at all, they may keep the watermarked version of the work, but THIS IS NOT A LEGITIMATE REASON FOR GETTING A REFUND.
